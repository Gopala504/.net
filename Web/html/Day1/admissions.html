How to Apply

  1. Choose your program from the Courses page.
  2. Complete the online application form.
  3. Submit required documents: transcripts, ID, photos.
  4. Pay the application fee.
  5. Await admission decision via email.

Admission Requirements

  • High school transcript for undergraduate applicants
  • Bachelor's transcript for postgraduate applicants
  • Proof of English proficiency (if applicable)
